Getting started

How do I turn on AdHush?

Open the app and tap the big black power button. iOS will ask once: "Allow AdHush to add VPN configurations?" Tap Allow and authenticate with Face ID. You're done.

Is it really a VPN? Will my data go through a server?

No. AdHush runs a local-only VPN. The "tunnel" lives entirely on your phone — there is no remote server. The only outbound traffic is DNS queries forwarded to Cloudflare 1.1.1.1 (or your chosen resolver) for domains that are NOT on the blocklist.

Does AdHush slow down my phone?

No. DNS lookups are fast (~10 ms typical). Web traffic itself bypasses the AdHush tunnel entirely via excludedRoutes — only DNS is intercepted.

Subscription & trial

How does the 7-day free trial work?

Apple charges nothing for the first 7 days. On day 7 it auto-charges $0.99 (or local equivalent) for the first month. You can cancel anytime during the trial and keep access until day 7 with no charge.

How do I cancel?

iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → AdHush → Cancel Subscription. Or open AdHush → Settings → Manage subscription, which deep-links you to the same page.

I bought it on another device — how do I restore?

Open AdHush → Settings → Restore purchases. Or tap "Restore purchases" on the paywall.

Can I share with my family?

Yes. AdHush Premium supports Family Sharing. One subscription covers everyone in your Apple Family group.

Troubleshooting

A website I need is broken when AdHush is on

Some sites pull required content from domains that are also used for tracking. You can:

  • Toggle AdHush off temporarily via the power button on the Home screen.
  • Add the domain to your local whitelist (Settings → Whitelist) in a future release.
  • Email us the URL — we curate the blocklist to minimize site-breakage.

The counter shows 0 even though blocking seems to work

If you upgraded from a build before App Group registration completed, the tunnel can't write counters to shared storage. Open Settings → Manage subscription → uninstall and reinstall. If the issue persists, email us.

iOS says "VPN configuration cannot be saved"

Restart your iPhone. If the error persists, delete any existing AdHush VPN profile in iOS Settings → General → VPN & Device Management, then enable from inside AdHush again.

How do I know it's actually blocking?

Run adblock-tester.com on Safari with AdHush on. AdHush typically scores 90%+.

Blocklist

Where does the blocklist come from?

AdHush ships with a unified blocklist compiled from HaGeZi Multi-Pro, OISD Big, StevenBlack hosts, and NextDNS OEM tracking. Around 520,000 unique domains, refreshed with each app update.

Can I add my own rules?

Not in v1. We're adding a custom-rules feature in a future release.
