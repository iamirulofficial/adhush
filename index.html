Block ads, trackers, and pop-ups across your iPhone.

AdHush is a privacy-first iOS app that filters every ad and tracker request — in Safari and inside every app — through a local on-device VPN. No account. No analytics. Nothing ever leaves your phone.

How it works

AdHush installs a local-only VPN profile on your device. The VPN intercepts every DNS lookup, refuses the ones pointing at known ad or tracker hosts, and forwards everything else to your chosen upstream resolver (Cloudflare 1.1.1.1 by default).

Because blocking happens at the DNS layer, AdHush works everywhere — Safari, Chrome, native apps, OEM telemetry — not just in one browser.

What you get with Premium

  • System-wide blocking — Local on-device VPN filters every ad and tracker, Safari and inside apps.
  • Safari content blocker — Browser-native rules pre-compiled to bytecode. Faster pages, no element shifting.
  • Zero telemetry — No account, no analytics. AdHush cannot see your traffic — by design.
